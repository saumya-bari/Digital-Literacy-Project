## Part B: Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

### General Online Safety
1. Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for all important accounts.  
2. Verify email sender addresses carefully.  
3. Check URLs for correct spelling and "https://".  
4. Avoid clicking unknown links in messages or emails.  
5. Use strong, unique passwords for each account.  
6. Do not open suspicious attachments.  

### Financial & UPI Safety
7. Always verify UPI payee name before sending money.  
8. Remember: UPI PIN is only for sending money, not receiving.  
9. Never share OTP, PIN, or banking details.  
10. Do not approve unknown payment requests.  
11. Set daily transaction limits in your banking app.  

### Device & Network Security
12. Avoid public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive logins.  
13. Keep your phone and apps updated regularly.  
14. Install apps only from official app stores.  
15. Use antivirus/security apps for protection.  
16. Always log out from shared devices.  

### Behavioral Awareness
17. Think before clicking any urgent message.  
18. Avoid offers that seem too good to be true.  
19. Do not share personal details publicly.  
20. Educate friends and classmates about cyber safety.
